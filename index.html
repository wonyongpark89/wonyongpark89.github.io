Dr. Alex Carter

Assistant Professor of Computational Linguistics at the University of Example. My work focuses on the intersection of neural networks and sociolinguistics.

Research Interests

Current Focus Cross-lingual Semantic Mapping

Investigating how large language models encode cultural nuances across low-resource languages.

Secondary Focus Ethics in AI Architecture

Developing frameworks for bias mitigation in generative pre-trained transformers.
