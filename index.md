### Biography
I am a researcher focused on the intersection of <span class="highlight">Natural Language Processing</span> and human cognition. My work aims to make machine learning models more interpretable and efficient for low-resource languages. Currently, I lead the *Linguistic AI Lab* where we bridge the gap between structural linguistics and deep learning.

---

### Research Interests
* **Interpretability:** Understanding neural attention mechanisms.
* **Low-Resource NLP:** Cross-lingual transfer learning.
* **Cognitive Modeling:** Simulating human language acquisition.
