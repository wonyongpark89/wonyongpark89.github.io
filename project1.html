Research Project

Networking for Disaster Education Research

Duration 2022 - 2023
Funder Economic and Social Research Council
Partners Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology
Ewha Womans University
Download

Summary

This project brought together disaster researchers, educators, survivors, and bereaved families from the UK and South Korea to facilitate dialogues and collective learning about disaster education.

In January 2022, we hosted a two-day conference in Southampton with speakers, discussants, and an audience from both countries. In January 2024, a webinar on education and disaster justice focused on the Grenfell Tower fire, the Hillsborough disaster, and the Sewol Ferry disaster. Project members also contributed to Disaster Haggyo (2022–2024), an interdisciplinary, field-based summer school for the study of disasters, as instructors and participants.

Key activities

  • Disaster Education for a Just and Resilient Future: An International Conference (Southampton, January 2022).
  • Education and Memorialisation for Disaster Justice: Webinar (January 2024).
  • Disaster Haggyo (2022–2024): interdisciplinary, field-based summer school for disaster studies.
