International Conference on Disaster Education and Memorialisation

Duration 2025 - 2027
Funder 4.16 Institute for Life and Safety Education
Partners 4.16 Institute for Life and Safety Education
Download
  • 2025 Conference Programme

Summary

Since 2025, we have been co-organising an annual international conference series focused on disaster education and memorialisation. The conference brings together officials, educators, researchers, and disaster-affected communities to support dialogue, reflection, and collective learning.

In the inaugural conference (April 2025), keynote speakers from the UK, Australia, and South Korea shared experience of education, archiving, and social justice after major disasters, alongside sessions led by practitioners and community members.

Objectives

  • Co-create an annual space for international exchange on disaster education and memorialisation.
  • Connect educators, policymakers, researchers, and disaster communities to share practices and priorities.
  • Surface lessons about justice, accountability, and remembrance after disasters, and translate them into educational practice.
  • Strengthen long-term partnerships between the 4.16 Institute and the University of Southampton around disaster education.
