Research Projects

2024 — 2026 | Funder: University of Southampton

Education and Memorialisation for Disaster Justice

This project investigates how societies remember past tragedies through education. By comparing the pedagogical responses to the Grenfell Tower, Hillsborough, and Sewol Ferry disasters, we explore how curricula can be designed to foster social justice, collective memory, and community resilience in the wake of unnatural disasters.

2023 — 2025 | Funder: ESRC

Making Research Count in Science Education Policy

An interdisciplinary initiative aimed at bridging the gap between academic research and national science education policy. This project develops frameworks for evidence-based decision-making, ensuring that the latest insights into the nature of science and scientific inquiry directly inform teacher education and school curriculum standards.

2025 — Present | Funder: Routledge Research

Disaster Education for Social Justice

Focusing on a STEM-centred approach, this project explores the integration of hazard literacy into the science classroom. We move beyond "technological fixes" to examine how slow learning and critical thinking about systemic risks can empower students to advocate for safer, more equitable futures in their communities.
